Does EndFrame require downloading an application?

No, EndFrame is a webapp that does not require downloading an application. You can connect easily with the browser on your device.

Is there a list of supported browsers?

Every updated browser is supported. Including all well known browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Brave and etc.

How do you start a meeting in EndFrame?

You can easily start a meeting by entering the synod ID or else by creating a new meeting using create synod ID button on the main page of the webapp.

What does peer-to-peer mean?

Peer-to-peer system creates a ecosystem with the two peers as their own server and client eliminating a host server or a middle man.

Is there a limitation for number of participants?

Currently, 10 people are the most that may attend a single meeting but is not limited by the services but rather by the bandwidth limitation.

How do the meetings relate to one another?

Any ongoing meetings won't be affected if one meeting room malfunctions. Every meeting has its own ecosystem in accordance to peer-to-peer infrastructure.

Does EndFrame save users' data and use it for other purposes?

We do not save or review users' data. So you can securely connect with your loved ones.
